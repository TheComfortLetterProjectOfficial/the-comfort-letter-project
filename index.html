The Comfort Letter Project

Handwritten hope for hospital patients and families during difficult times.

💙 Our Mission

We are a student-led initiative dedicated to sending handwritten letters of encouragement to hospital patients and families, bringing comfort, hope, and emotional support when it is needed most.

✍️ How It Works

  1. Students and volunteers sign up
  2. They write handwritten comfort letters
  3. Letters are collected and organized
  4. We deliver them to hospitals and care centers

🤝 Get Involved

You can help by writing letters, starting a school group, or partnering with us to spread kindness.
Write Letters Become a Volunteer

💛 Impact

Our goal is to build a growing network of student volunteers who deliver thousands of handwritten letters each year to bring emotional support to patients and families in hospitals and care facilities.

📩 Join the Project

Sign up to become a volunteer and start writing letters.

🏫 Start a Chapter

The Comfort Letter Project grows through student-led chapters in schools and communities. Each chapter organizes volunteers who write handwritten letters of encouragement for hospital patients and families.

Chapters help expand the mission locally while staying connected to the larger project, creating a network of students committed to spreading kindness and emotional support.

📌 How to Start

  1. Gather a small group of students interested in volunteering
  2. Choose a chapter leader or coordinator
  3. Begin writing comfort letters as a team
  4. Submit or deliver letters through the main project system
